About me

I received Ph.D. degree in computer science and technology from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), China, in 2018. I am currently an associate professor at the State Key Laboratory of Networking and Switching Technology, BUPT. My current research interest includes mobile computing, crowdsensing, and vehicular networks.
